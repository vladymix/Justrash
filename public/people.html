Hot spots

This tab is the main place of social data. We use full trash cans concentrations for knowing where have big amounts of people been.
An example would be, after a football match, look at the nearby trash cans to discover where have people gone (maybe to a street with lots of restaurants, which may indicate the potential of food-related businesses in that area).
